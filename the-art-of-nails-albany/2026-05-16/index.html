01

What 2026 is telling us

Web research conducted 2026-05-16. Sources: Refinery29, NewBeauty, NZ Herald Viva, Marie Claire, Glazeme. Findings synthesised into the colour palette card and the showcase post sequencing.

Quiet luxury, not maximalism
"Elevated simplicity" is the defining aesthetic of 2026 nails — clean, luxurious, thoughtfully designed. Luxury minimalism leans into beige, champagne, creamy whites; calm, high-end environments built with fewer but higher-quality materials.
Marie Claire · Refinery29 · 2026
Chrome & glass finishes dominate
Mirror, chrome, metallic overlays and glass-like finishes are everywhere this season — reflective, multi-dimensional, high-shine. Already in Albany's bank: Pink Chrome, Russian Manicure with gold dot detail.
Glazeme · Refinery29 · 2026
Russian manicure is the technique of the year
"Russian manicures continue to dominate salons thanks to ultra-precise cuticle work and long-lasting gel finish" — Glazeme 2026 guide. Soft square, oval and almond are the shapes; technique is the differentiator. Albany already runs this as a signature service.
Glazeme · Bangstyle · 2026
Four colours leading the season
Iced mocha · Oxblood / Rich Conker (the "new neutral") · Milky pink ("ultimate soft luxury") · Champagne chrome. These map directly to Albany's existing photos (burgundy / nude / chrome) — the palette card surfaces this as a curatorial signal.
NewBeauty · Who What Wear · Bustle · 2026
02

What this feed does, and avoids

Owner brief: "make it look organic, not a gallery". Below is the literal translation of that brief into rules we hold every post to.

What this feed does

  • One subject per frame — no collages or stamps
  • Six photographs share a single editorial preset — contrast, restraint, slight warmth
  • Three editorial cards weave through to break the gallery feel
  • Palette card surfaces trend curation, not decoration
  • Service card highlights the technique the feed centres on
  • Quote card anchors the brand voice mid-feed
  • Cool / mid / warm tone alternation per row

What this feed avoids

  • "Gallery dump" — nine identical crops, same lighting, same angle
  • Decorative monogram tile — drops it (no real content added)
  • Warm Instagram-orange filters, oversaturated edits
  • Text overlays on the photograph itself
  • Promo / sale / "DM now 💅" language
  • Watermarks — until parent-brand asset lands (Phase 3)
  • Generic stock-photo composition
